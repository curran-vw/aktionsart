About Aktionsart

About Andre Becker, the Berlin-based enterprise marketing consultant behind Aktionsart, and the editorial standards behind the 2026 enterprise call tracking report.

About Andre Becker

Andre Becker is a Berlin-based enterprise marketing consultant. He advises in-house performance teams at mid-market and enterprise firms. The work spans DACH and the United Kingdom. The focus is contribution margin per channel, multi-touch attribution, and the operational side of paid-media spend. His career has been split between in-house roles at consumer-services brands and consulting work with enterprise marketing-ops teams.

Aktionsart is where Andre publishes his work on the call tracking and attribution market. The site exists for one reason. Most enterprise call tracking reviews start at the feature matrix. A working performance team's actual decision starts somewhere else. It starts at the unit economics. It starts at the contribution margin per channel. It starts at the operational cost of running the platform week to week. The reviews here are written for that reader.

Editorial standards

Every review on this site is researched first-hand. Platforms are tested with real Google Ads campaigns and routed inbound calls. Operator interviews are conducted on background. Quotes are paraphrased by default. Identifiers are redacted by default. The four-axis scoring rubric is published on the methodology page. It is applied consistently across every platform reviewed, including the editorial pick.

What the site will not do

This site does not run sponsored reviews. It does not accept payment to upgrade a score. It does not review platforms the editor has not tested or interviewed operators about. The reviews are not generated from press releases, vendor decks, or analyst reports.

What the site will do

The site cites authoritative sources for technical claims. Google's documentation is linked where relevant. So are Wikipedia entries on attribution and call tracking. Schema.org markup specs are linked where they help a reader. Industry data from Gartner, Forrester, and the Interactive Advertising Bureau is cited where relevant. Operator interview quotes are flagged inline when used.

How this site makes money

Aktionsart is reader-supported. The site earns a referral fee when readers sign up for tools through affiliate links. Commissions do not influence the rankings. The methodology is applied identically to every platform reviewed. That includes the editorial pick.

The site is run by an independent editorial entity. It is not owned by, employed by, or affiliated with CallScaler. The same is true for the other products it reviews. The relationship is the standard affiliate-link relationship. The editor takes corrections seriously. Vendor updates, factual disputes, and methodology questions all run through the contact page.

How to reach Andre

For corrections, vendor updates, or methodology questions, see the contact page. Andre also writes occasional long-form pieces. Topics include contribution-margin frameworks and multi-touch attribution model selection. Those are published on the same site under the methodology section.
