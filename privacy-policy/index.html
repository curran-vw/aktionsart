Privacy policy

Privacy policy for Aktionsart. What is collected, how it is used, and the rights of EU, UK, and California residents.

Last updated May 1, 2026.

Information we collect

This privacy notice describes how Aktionsart collects, uses, and shares information when you visit this website.

  • Standard server logs (IP address, user agent string, referrer header) retained for up to thirty days.
  • If analytics is enabled, a privacy-respecting provider that does not set cross-site cookies and does not transfer data outside the EU.
  • Email correspondence retained as needed to respond and follow up.

What we do not collect

No third-party advertising trackers. No Facebook Pixel. No Google Ads remarketing. No data sales. No fingerprinting beyond what the standard server logs already capture.

Cookies

The site does not set marketing or tracking cookies by default. Any cookie used is strictly necessary for site function. EU consent requirements are met by avoiding non-essential cookies.

Affiliate links

Affiliate links share a referral identifier and a sign-up confirmation with the vendor. The site does not receive your personal account data from the vendor.

Your rights

EU, UK, and California residents have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. Email editor@aktionsart.org to exercise them. Response within thirty days.

Children

This site is not directed to children under thirteen.

Changes

Material changes will be flagged at the top of this page for at least thirty days.
