Contact

Contact the editor

How to reach Andre Becker for corrections, vendor updates, or methodology questions on the 2026 enterprise call tracking report.

Editorial corrections and vendor updates

Email editor@aktionsart.org with three things. Send the URL of the page in question. Send what you believe is incorrect. Send a source for verification. Corrections are reviewed within two business days. Vendor updates are evaluated against the published methodology rubric.

Press and partnerships

For press inquiries, email press@aktionsart.org. The site does not accept sponsored content. It does not accept sponsored reviews. It does not accept guest posts.

Methodology questions

If you would like to replicate any of the testing, the test artifacts are available on request. The artifacts include test campaigns, landing pages, and per-platform setup logs. Email the editor with the page URL and the methodology question.

Reader notes

Reader notes are welcome. So are suggestions for the next refresh. So are pointers to platforms worth covering next year. Email editor@aktionsart.org.

No contact form by design. Email is the fastest way to reach the editor.
